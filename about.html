The Story So Far

Shale-PIZZAAsss is famous for Pizzas, Pasta food. Shale-PIZZAAsss was opened with one restaurant in the year 1960 and now it has more than 1000 restaurant across the countries.

Shale Pizza was one of the first fast-food chains to expand internationally, opening outlets in England, Mexico and Jamaica by the mid-1960s. Throughout the 1970s and 80s, Shale Pizza experienced mixed success domestically, as it went through a series of changes in corporate ownership with little or no experience in the restaurant business. In the early 1970s, Shale Pizza was sold to the spirits distributor Heublein, which was taken over by the R.J. Reynolds food and tobacco conglomerate, which later sold the chain to PepsiCo. The chain continued to expand overseas, and in 1987 Shale Pizza became the first Western restaurant chain to open in China
